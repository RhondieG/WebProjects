Hurrican Dorian and it's Path of Destruction

Hurricane Dorian has destroyed the Island of Bahamas

Hurricane Dorian made landfall on the Abaco Islands, then hitting the Bahamas on August 31, 2019. The Category 5 storm continued to churn northwestward, but hovered over the Bahams for a steady 36 hours, with little movement. The storm sustained winds of 185 mph. Gusts over 200 mph were being experienced there while the storm surged up to and over 20 feet. Dorian was second only to Hurricane Allen in 1980, with its 190mph winds.
